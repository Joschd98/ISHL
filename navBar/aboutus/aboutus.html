About us

We're a small team of Master's students at Saarland University who love learning — and helping kids love it too!

We created ArithmaKids to make math practice fun, simple, and exciting. Instead of boring worksheets, kids get to learn through play and small wins that build confidence.

We work with teachers and child learning experts to make sure ArithmaKids is joyful, effective, and easy to use.

Because we believe: kids don't hate math – they just need the right way to practice it!
